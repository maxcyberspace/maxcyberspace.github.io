Google Chrome is faster than Mozilla Firefox

Published on: Nov 29, 2025

I ditched Google Chrome after they blocked the ublock origin extension. I cannot imagine now to surf the internet without an adblocker that’s why I switched fully to Mozilla Firefox on all my devices.

And today after 4-5 months, I used Google Chrome because some website wasn’t working as intended and I have found that Chrome is faster than Firefox. I was using this Chrome build for a year before switching to Firefox. But it is still faster than Firefox and I have also noticed that the sites work better and load faster on Chrome as compared to Firefox.

No doubt they have highest number of users. I am not planning to use Google Chrome as I have mentioned earlier that it is impossible for me to use internet without adblocker.
