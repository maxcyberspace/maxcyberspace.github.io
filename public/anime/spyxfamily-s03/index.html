SpyxFamily Season 3

Published on: Jan 28, 2026

I have finally watched the Season 3 completely and I have a word or two to write about it. The starting episodes were good, but I didn’t enjoyed the middle ones. The last episodes gave the true spy anime vibes. I liked the humor in the ending episodes more. But I have to say that I didn’t felt that much awed or excited while watching it I mean I was excited and waiting for the studio to drop this season but it didn’t matched my expectation. I think the comedy and goofy moments in this season can’t be compared with the previous seasons. Or I am just living in nostalgia?!?!?!

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐/5
