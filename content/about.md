I am Max, a Pharmacy student from India. I am in the last year of my graduation, apart from completing assignments, taking tests and working on projects I like to work on my website in my free time. This website is like a personal corner for me where I share my thoughts and interests on various topics. Generally, you will find the content related to food, anime and technology on this website.

I made this website in July, 2025 and it went online in Aug, 2025. Before publishing my website on Neocities, I was writing blogs on WordPress and before that on Blogger. I found those platforms convenient but they didn't provide the customizability and had pretty over-designed themes for my taste. I find it very surprising that this website survived seven months, usually I get bored and nuke the project because I lose interest after some time.
